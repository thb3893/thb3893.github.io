TRAINING GUIDE

Weight training is not just about building muscle; it's about developing discipline, boosting confidence, and enhancing overall health. Below, you'll find my personal favorite exercises for each muscle group.

CHEST

  • Flat Bench Press
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
  • Iso-Lateral Chest Press Machine

BACK

  • Pull Ups
  • Iso-Lateral Row Machine
  • T-Bar Row

SHOULDERS

  • Overhead Shoulder Press
  • Lateral Raises
  • Rear Delt Fly

TRICEPS

  • Dips
  • Single Arm Catanas
  • Overhead Extensions

BICEPS

  • Single Arm Preacher Curls
  • Hammer Curls
  • Barbell Curls
  • Close-Grip Underhand Lat Pulldowns

LEGS

  • Squats
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Leg Extensions & Curls
  • Standing Calf Press

CORE

  • Hanging Leg/Knee Raises
  • Weighted Sit Ups
  • Suitcase Carries
  • L-Sits

FOREARMS

  • Sam Sulek Curls
  • Reverse Curls
